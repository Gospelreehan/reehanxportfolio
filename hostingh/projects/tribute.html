About Kamal Haasan

Kamal Haasan is an Indian film actor, director, screenwriter, producer, and playback singer who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. Over his career, he has been recognized for his work in various Indian languages including Hindi, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, and Bengali. Kamal Haasan made his debut as a child artist in the 1959 Tamil film “Kalathur Kannamma”, which won him the President’s Gold Medal.

He is considered one of the finest actors in Indian cinema, known for his versatile roles in films such as “Anbe sivam”, “Vishwaroopam”, and “Mahanadhi”. He has won several National Film Awards and Filmfare Awards for his exceptional acting and contributions to the industry.
